Protecting India's Coastline with Predictive Analytics

ArcGX is a pioneering startup proud to present Neithal Kavasam, our flagship project designed to address the critical challenge of coastal vulnerability. Our mission is to transform how maritime stakeholders manage coastal assets by shifting the paradigm from reactive repairs to proactive, data-driven resilience.

The Neithal Kavasam platform synthesizes complex environmental data into a simple, actionable risk score using our core innovation, the Coastal Vulnerability Index (CVI). This allows port authorities, industrial plants, and coastal communities to identify, monitor, and predict erosion hotspots with unprecedented accuracy, empowering them to protect critical infrastructure and enhance operational safety.

The Critical Information Gap for Coastal Managers

Problem 1: Data is Too Broad or Too Slow

Government data provides a national overview but lacks the real-time, high-resolution detail needed for specific operational decisions. Conversely, private surveys are project-based, expensive, and provide only a static snapshot in time, failing to capture continuous change.

Problem 2: Lack of Actionable Intelligence

Raw geospatial data‚Äîmaps, charts, and tables‚Äîrequires expert interpretation. A non-technical port manager lacks a simple tool that clearly states: "This 200m section of your property is at high risk of erosion within the next six months." This leads to reactive, costly emergency repairs.

Core Innovation: The Coastal Vulnerability Index (CVI)

The CVI is our unique, algorithmically calculated risk score. This radar chart visualizes the key factors contributing to vulnerability, allowing for quick comparison between different coastal sections.

Our Business Plan: A Subscription-Based Model

We will operate on a B2B/B2G Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model, offering tiered annual subscriptions. This provides clients with continuous data updates, platform improvements, and dedicated support without a large upfront capital expenditure.

Our Execution Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation & MVP (Months 1-6)

  • Finalize company registration and procure essential software.
  • Develop backend data pipeline for satellite imagery.
  • Create Version 1.0 of the CVI algorithm.
  • Build the basic user dashboard and conduct pilot drone surveys.
  • Demonstrate a working MVP to stakeholders.

Phase 2: Pilot Client & Enhancement (Months 7-12)

  • Engage a pilot client (e.g., a local port) for a free trial.
  • Gather extensive user feedback to refine UI/UX.
  • Integrate the predictive analytics model for erosion forecasting.
  • Develop a detailed case study quantifying the value provided.
  • Finalize the subscription pricing model.

Phase 3: Scale-Up & Commercialization (Months 13-24)

  • Launch sales and marketing campaigns to acquire paying customers.
  • Expand service coverage to other key coastal states.
  • Hire key personnel for business development and drone operations.
  • Develop the "On-Demand Drone Scan" feature within the platform.
  • Scale up cloud infrastructure to handle multiple clients.
